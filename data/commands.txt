FULL LIST OF COMMANDS:
help - show this message
quit - quit the game
end_turn - end your turn
show_board - show the board
show_hand - show your hand
show_opponent - show opponent's health
show_player - show your health
play <card_name> - play a card from your hand
attack <attacker_id> <defender_id> - attack with a minion
go_face <attacker_id> - attack the opponent with a minion
